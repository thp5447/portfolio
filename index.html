About Me

Hi, I'm Travis Perry, a Computer Science and Mathematics major at New York University's Courant Institute, graduating a semester early. Originally from Fairfax, Virginia, I'm passionate about combining my technical skills, analytical mindset, and problem-solving abilities to tackle real-world challenges. I'm seeking a full-time opportunity where I can apply my technical foundation in software development and data analysis, contribute to impactful, real-world projects, and continue growing into a leadership-oriented role that bridges technology and strategic decision-making.
